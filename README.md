This is a simple game which I made for a University module. The player controls a 17th century sailing ship and must battle against other ships controlled by AI.

CONTROLS

WASD - move (W and S for moving forward/backward, A and D for steering)\
Mouse cursor - look around with the camera. Your cannons will also attempt to aim towards the cursor\
- Note: cannons can't aim towards the cursor if the angle's too far. E.g. a ship with cannons on its left and right sides can't aim them directly in front of or behind it
- When cannons are being aimed properly, white lines will appear on the water to show you the cannons' range
 
Q and E - rotate the camera by 90 degrees (for if some sort of terrain is blocking your view)\
Left click - fire your cannons (if being aimed properly)\
Right click - hold to use a spyglass, allowing you to see further\
Space - activate your special attack. Each ship class has a different special, which are listed below:
- Cutter - drops a mine behind it, which explodes when hit by a ship, cannonball or other exploding mine
- Brigantine - charges forward with a ram, damaging the first ship it hits
- Frigate - Fires a mortar at the mouse cursor, dealing damage over a short duration (spyglass must be open in order to fire)
- Galleon - Fires multiple inaccurate shots from all cannons for a short duration

Tab - hold to show a scoreboard containing every player's status\
Esc - pause/unpause\
P - Hold for 5 seconds to automatically win and unlock the next level
